Camel

Interesting Facts: • The Camel's ears are furry. • The camel can easily across the sand because of its specifically designed feet. • When they find water, they drink as much as possible.

Chameleon

Interesting Facts: • Chameleons are repltiles that are part of the iguana suborder. • Changing skinf color is an important part among chameleons. • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Cicada

Interesting Facts: • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs. • Most have red-orange eyes. • Females may be attracted to the sound of motors.

Panda

Interesting Facts: • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim. • Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day. • An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!

Pigeon

Interesting Facts: • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals. • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities. • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Teddy Bear

Interesting Facts: • The teddy bear was named after the American president Theodore Roosevelt. • A teddy bear is what you use to cuddle when you sleep. • A teddy bear is made out of cotton in the inside and it is very fluffy!
